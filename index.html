Frequently Asked Questions

Arizona allows 33% VLT on front side windows and any darkness on rear windows. We ensure all installations meet state regulations.

Most sedan installations take 2-3 hours, while SUVs and trucks typically require 3-4 hours for complete installation.

Our lifetime warranty covers bubbling, peeling, discoloration, and adhesive failure. Manufacturer warranty protects against film defects.

Wait 3-7 days before cleaning. Use ammonia-free cleaners and soft cloths. Avoid scraping or sharp objects on the film.

Our signal-friendly films won't interfere with GPS, cellular, or radio signals. Defrosters work normally through quality tint.

We primarily work from our climate-controlled facility for best results, but mobile service is available for fleet customers.
